The BSE Sensex, also known as the Bombay Stock Exchange Sensitive Index, is a stock market index of the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) in India. It comprises the top 30 stocks listed on the BSE based on market capitalization. The BSE Sensex is considered to be one of the most prominent stock market indices in India and is often used as a barometer of the Indian economy.
